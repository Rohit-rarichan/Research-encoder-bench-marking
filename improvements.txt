     - Especially useful for ResNet101, SegFormer

   Current: All models use regular grid convolutions
   What to do:

     - Apply to bottleneck areas
   Expected improvement: +0.03-0.07 mIoU

   Current: ConvNeXt has some channel attention, others don't
   What to do:
     - Add SE blocks after each major feature extraction block
     - Re-weight channels based on importance
     - Minimal parameter overhead
   Expected improvement: +0.01-0.04 mIoU
   Difficulty: Very Low
